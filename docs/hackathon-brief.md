## Tracks

1. **Athlete Performance & Coaching** — AI/analytics for training, injury prevention, biomechanics, motion tracking, video analysis
2. **Fan Experience & Engagement** — fantasy, predictions, gamification, personalized content, interactive viewing
3. **Media, Content & Broadcasting** — AI commentary, automated highlights, multilingual broadcasting, media workflows
4. **Sports Business & Operations** — leagues, venues, ticketing, scheduling, scouting, sponsorships, analytics
5. **Open Innovation** — any AI-powered sports product

Any sport is eligible (cricket, football/soccer, basketball, tennis, F1, esports, …).

## Featured / sponsor tech

- **Lyzr** — production multi-agent AI workflows
- **Qdrant** — vector search: multimodal sports data, semantic retrieval, video search, AI memory
- **Enkrypt AI** — genAI guardrails (hallucination, prompt injection, unsafe output, data leakage)
- **InsForge** — AI infrastructure / developer tools; has a dedicated prize track

Any additional models/APIs/frameworks are allowed.
